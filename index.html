BAD BITCH BIKER CLUB

B.B.B.C. is a collection of 10,000 unique hand-drawn bad bitch NFT’s living on the Etherium block chain. Each bad bitch biker girl is based on a random generation created from our selection of 311 different traits, including clothes, helmets, bikes, expressions and more. all of our bitches are bad, but some are the baddest of them all.

When you buy bad bitch you are not only owning a piece of art or avatar. you are gaining access to our exclusive club, where we will showcase new projects, offers and merchandise. not to mention our bike racing game where you can play as one of our girls.
