Bad Bitch Biker Club is a notorious motorcycle gang based in Cali, made up of some of the hottest outlaw biker chicks, operating both illegal and legal businesses. They combine Gun Running, Drug Running and a hang out at hot chick chopper bikes garage. Join the gang to embark on adventures of thrill and excitement. They take no prisoners! What role in the gang will you play?

Raven is the President, aka the highest-ranking member of the Bad bitch Biker Club. She serves as the figurehead and final decision-maker of the club. Raven leads and guides the club through each evolution all the while serving as a rock to all members of the club.

Dagger is a long servant of the Bad Bitch Biker Club earning her high rank as Vice President. She supervises all business plans for the club and coordinates with other rivals to make sure they aren’t interfering with the club’s business. She has strong relationships with every club member and relays any intel to the Club President. She has the power to authorise assassinations if necessary.

Diva is the Treasurer and money laundering is her forte. She handles all the dirty cash and revenue that comes in and out of the Bad Bitch Biker Club. Diva is on the FBI’s most wanted list for being suspected of laundering over $250 million worth of narcotics in a deal made with the Sinaloa Cartel.

Panther is known for her strategic planning. She’s played a part in some of the world’s largest drug and arms smuggling operations and has connections with border officials who assist in the crime the club commits. Panther will research, plan, and organise all runs that the club carries out and has overall authority on a club ride when a president is not in attendance.

Foxy is head of law enforcement within the club and will not hesitate to hand out bounties against anyone who dares break the law on the club’s territory. She has US governors on the club’s payroll who keep her updated with the latest intel the government has on the club. These are handy connections as she’s been on the Most Wanted List for over 20 years for suspected drug running, and suspicion of murder as well as having links with the Mexican Cartel.

Widow Maker is the protection of the club and is always down for a shootout. She handles the Bad Bitch Biker Club’s entire weaponry arsenal. Distributing guns and ammo to all of the patch holders and protects the club's reputation in any type of conflict or rampage.

Kitty is one of the most innocent looking biker chicks but don’t be fooled by her innocent looks. She is the mastermind behind the vice president and president's plans for the gang. Orchestrating and organising the club’s criminal activities.

Hornet a notorious outlaw. She is responsible for ensuring all riders in the group remain as a unit during operations or missions. She carries heavy weaponry and will fire bullets towards rivals who disturb their route when on the move.

Plague is a quiet character with loud actions. Notoriously known for her brutal ability to assassinate and execute the masses. Rumor has it she once wiped out an entire rival biker gang with her signature mace weapon. Reaper was later killed in a gang fight and rose from the dead rejoining the gang as one of its most lethal members.

The club was formed by a massacre event where the bad bitch members wiped out their husbands and partners. After decades of being members of a notorious biker club run by their husbands and partners, they were used and abused to the point where they’d had enough and decided to take matters into their own hands. One night after months of planning the bad bitches murdered all male members of the club, renaming it to the Bad Bitch Biker Club. They are now the most dangerous outlaw motorcycle gang in the world.
